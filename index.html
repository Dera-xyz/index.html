30 minutes recipe for Efo Riro

Efo riro is a simple to make yoruba delicacy. It requires minimal incgredients and a short number of steps to make. It used pepper, iru and the efo leaves as the base. Every other thing is an add on.
See, I told you, easy to make. Interesting story, I made it for the first time ever yesterday and I can say it is the best meal I have ever made. Just to show you it is quick and easy to make

Recipe Information

  • Preparation time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking time: 15 minutes
  • Number of servings: 3
  • Difficulty level: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 200g of efo leaves
  • 100g of pepper mix
  • 1 Onion bulb
  • 2 pieces of ponmo (optional)
  • Proten if choice (goat meat was used for this recipe)
  • 3 pieces of smoken panla (stock fish can be used)
  • Palm oil

Instructions

  1. Wash your goat meat and boil for 5 minutes
  2. Add salt, seasoning cubes, diced onions and part of the pepper mix to taste
  3. Leave for another 10 minutes to cook
  4. Bring down once water is almost dry (do not allow water to dry)
  5. Put a dose of palm oil and diced onions and pepper mix to fry
  6. Add diced ponmo and smoked panla
  7. Add your goat meat and stock
  8. Add salt and seasoning cubes to taste
  9. Add diced efo leaves and stir until all the laaves are soaked
  10. Leave on low heat to cook for 10 minutes

Food is Ready!

Note: Ugwu (spinach) leaves can be used to replace Efo but it would not taste as good. Also rememeber not to use too much of the stock water as it will amke the soup too watery. Less is more!
